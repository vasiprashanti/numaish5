Visitor Guidelines

Do's Don'ts
Carry multiple payment options including cash and digital payments.Avoid carrying valuables such as expensive jewelry and unnecessary gadgets.
Keep personal items such as wallets and mobile phones secure.Do not litter; use designated waste disposal bins.
Use public transport like metro, cabs, or autos to avoid parking issues.Do not park in unauthorized areas to avoid fines or towing.
Supervise children and senior citizens closely in crowded areas.Do not engage in conflicts; maintain a polite demeanor.
Follow security and event guidelines, including entry/exit protocols.Do not ignore emergency exits; familiarize yourself with their locations.
